Giant Snore

JANUARY 11, 2026 • CHILDREN'S

Once upon a time there was a very peaceful village, where the crickets sang softly and the wind blew gently. But suddenly, everything changed. One night, a terrible noise began to frighten the villagers: SNORE! SNOOOORE! The ground shook and the windows rattled.

Everyone in the village was certain of one thing: it was the work of a giant. "He must be enormous and furious!" said the baker, hiding under the counter. "He must have teeth of stone!" whispered the teacher. The fear was so great — and the noise so loud — that no one could sleep a wink.

One day passed, two days... Three days! And every night the giant's enormous noise was heard. Nobody slept well in the village!

In the midst of so many frightened people, there was a young man named Pedro. He was very brave, but above all, he was dying of exhaustion. He wandered around yawning in corners, stumbling with fatigue. Fed up with all that racket, he decided: "I'm going up there to face that giant. I need to sleep!"

Pedro grabbed a lantern and followed the sound. He crossed the deserted village, went down the foggy hill, and began climbing the rocky mountain. The farther he walked, the louder the snoring grew. It sounded like thunder!

The sound led straight to a dark cave at the top of the mountain. Pedro swallowed his fear, readied his bravest voice, and entered the cave. But when he shone the lantern into the back, he had an incredible surprise. There was no giant at all.

There in the back, a tiny little mouse was sleeping, belly up. It turned out the cave had a special shape, like a great horn or an enormous flute. The mouse's tiny little snore bounced off the walls, echoed, and came out of the cave's mouth transformed into that thunderous noise.

Pedro gently nudged the little mouse. The tiny creature woke up startled, rubbing its eyes. Pedro explained the mix-up and saw that the mouse felt sad. "Oh, I'm sorry," said the mouse. "It's just that the cave is a little bit warmer than outside."

The young man then had an idea. He carefully picked up the mouse and said: "I have the solution for both of us." Pedro took his new friend home and prepared a little shoebox lined with soft, warm cotton.

That night, the little mouse slept snug and warm in the box and made no noise at all. Pedro and the village finally got to rest in peace once more.

Small Poems

MAY 12, 2025 • POETRY

1

When doubt arises,
pull that string hanging from the wall.
A canvas will unroll downward,
showing the image of a Chinese man seated in a bamboo grove.
"I" — he will tell us — "am also a skeptic."

2

When you know everything,
look upon a blue flower
forgotten among the roses.

3

FEBRUARY 21, 2019 • MICROFICTION

Photographs

It was the tenth house the couple had visited. Newlyweds, demanding and full of dreams. Inside, they found dozens of photographs crowding the walls. First suspicious, then astonished, they saw the photos were of themselves! Images of their children's births, their daughter's graduation, of dogs. It was the perfect house. Even when the husband saw a photo of himself, skeletal and pale, in a hospital bed, surrounded by grandchildren.

Work of Art

An entire squadron hovered near the strange obelisk that had appeared in the orbit of Venus. What the hell is that? barked Admiral Chang. A rhetorical question. Nobody knew. Every base was on alert while the nations debated what to do with the alien object, mysterious and still. Very far away, a curator on an unknown planet was worried. He had somehow lost the main piece of his collection.

Logo

The designer was exasperated. She had shown over a hundred logos, but the clients were never satisfied. They said: we want something colorful but not too cheerful; something sober but fun. She snapped: Do you realize what you want is contradictory? One of them answered: We serve a diverse clientele. We never know if they are happy or sad. Damn, never working for a funeral home again, she thought.
